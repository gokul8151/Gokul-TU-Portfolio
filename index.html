Steel Battalion

3D Tank Combat Game | Developed in Unity

Description

A 3D tank combat game built in Unity, where players control a heavy-duty war tank battling enemy tanks with realistic firing mechanics, explosions, and pursuit AI.

Your Role

Solo Developer - Design, programming, AI, shooting mechanics, VFX, and SFX.

Tools & Technologies

Unity, C#, Unity NavMesh, Particle System, Audio Mixer

Key Features

  • AI-powered enemy tanks that chase and shoot the player
  • Custom shooting system with explosions and sound effects
  • Realistic tank movement and damage feedback
  • Health bar system with visual and functional effects
